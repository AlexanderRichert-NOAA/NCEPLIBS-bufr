4 C> THIS SUBROUTINE UNPACKS AND RETURNS THE DESCRIPTORS
5 C> CONTAINED WITHIN SECTION 3 OF A BUFR MESSAGE STORED IN ARRAY MBAY.
6 C> THE START OF THE BUFR MESSAGE (I.E. THE STRING "BUFR") MUST BE
7 C> ALIGNED ON THE FIRST FOUR BYTES OF MBAY. NOTE ALSO THAT THIS
8 C> SUBROUTINE DOES NOT RECURSIVELY RESOLVE SEQUENCE DESCRIPTORS THAT
9 C> APPEAR WITHIN SECTION 3; RATHER, WHAT IS RETURNED IS THE EXACT LIST
10 C> OF DESCRIPTORS AS IT APPEARS WITHIN SECTION 3.
11 C>
